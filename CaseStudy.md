# Bake Hauss Case Study

## Introduction
The Bake Hauss project is a strategic digital transformation designed to transition a premium, home-based artisanal bakery into a scalable digital brand. Historically, the business operated within a manual paradigm—relying exclusively on fragmented phone-based coordination and word-of-mouth. 

Our objective is to bridge this digital gap by engineering a bespoke full-stack ecosystem. This transition from 'invisible' to 'accessible' allows the business to scale its reach and professionalize its operations without losing the 'home-baked' essence that customers value.

## Problem Statement
The existing business model relies entirely on offline communication, which limits visibility, customer reach, and operational efficiency. Without an online presence, potential customers cannot easily view past work, explore design options, or initiate contact conveniently. This lack of digital accessibility restricts growth opportunities and makes it difficult to compete with other local bakeries that have established online platforms.

## Objectives
1. Establish a professional online presence for the business.
2. Create a platform to display previously made cakes and designs.
3. Simplify the inquiry and ordering process through an online contact form.
4. Maintain the personalised, custom-order nature of the business.
5. Improve customer engagement through a blog and visual storytelling.

## Target Audience
The primary audience consists of local customers seeking custom cakes for personal events such as birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and corporate celebrations. The website is designed to appeal to individuals who value handcrafted, bespoke cakes and prefer a modern, user-friendly browsing experience.

## Project Scope
The Bake Hauss project involves the design, development, and deployment of a responsive website that includes the following key components:
* **Home Page**: Introduction to the brand and its offerings.
* **About Page**: Story of the team and the inspiration behind the business.
* **Product Page**: Visual showcase of past creations categorised by event type.
* **Contact Page**: Inquiry form for custom orders and direct communication details.

## Design Approach
The website design follows a modern and minimalist aesthetic, emphasizing high-quality visuals and intuitive navigation. The color palette and typography are chosen to reflect warmth, creativity, and professionalism. The layout prioritizes user experience, ensuring that visitors can easily browse the gallery, read blog posts, and submit inquiries without friction.

## Technology Stack
* **Frontend**: React.js
* **Backend**: Node.js & Express
* **Database**: Postgresql
* **Hosting**: Not decided yet

## Implementation Plan
1. **Research and Requirement Gathering**: Understanding the business needs and customer expectations.
2. **Wireframing and Design**: Creating mockups and visual prototypes.
3. **Development**: Building the website structure and integrating functionality.
4. **Testing**: Ensuring responsiveness, usability, and performance across devices.
5. **Deployment**: Hosting the website and connecting it to a custom domain.
6. **Maintenance**: Regular updates, content management, and performance monitoring.

## Expected Outcomes
* Enhanced brand visibility within the local community.
* Increased customer inquiries and engagement.
* Streamlined communication between the baker and clients.
* Improved customer satisfaction through easier access to information and inspiration.
* Foundation for future expansion, such as online ordering or e-commerce integration.

## Conclusion
Bake Hauss represents a step forward in bridging traditional craftsmanship with modern digital tools. By creating a dedicated online platform, the project not only supports the growth of a local business but also demonstrates how thoughtful web design can preserve authenticity while enhancing accessibility. The website will serve as both a marketing tool and a customer engagement hub, ensuring that the business continues to thrive in an increasingly digital marketplace.
